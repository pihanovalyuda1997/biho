    Hairpin Plant Stand

    • Description
    • Care Instruction
    • ﻿60cm high
    • Inner top diameter is 19cm
    • Comes with a 19cm Italian Terracotta Pot
    • Hand-crafted steel with matte black finish

    Hairpin Plant Stands are made of steel with a matte black finish, rendering a sleek and modern look. They look great while clustered with other plants in a focal point of your home, or placed besides your sofa, bookshelf or TV unit.

    Plant stands are great for bringing smaller plants into view and showing off plants with interesting foliage patterns, as well as giving a plant extra height while you wait for it to grow...

    The top inner diameter is 19cm and the height of the stand (without the pot) is 60cm.

    Spot clean any blemishes with a cloth, dishwashing liquid and warm water.
